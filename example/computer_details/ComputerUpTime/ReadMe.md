## Uptime Module
---
##### Author: [Zack McCauley](https://www.github.com/WardsParadox)
##### Idea: John Black @black on MacAdmins.slack


### Description:
Shows total uptime of computer in days. Tooltip shows last reboot date.
Click to show a prompt to restart.
